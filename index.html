In addition, he recorded 19 songs in Tamil and 16 songs in Hindi in a day, which has also been called a record.[16] In 2012, he received the state NTR National Award for his contributions to Indian cinema.[17] In 2016, he was honoured with the Silver Peacock Medal as Indian Film Personality of the Year.[18][19][20][21] He was a recipient of the Padma Shri (2001) and Padma Bhushan (2011) from the Government of India.[22] On 25 September 2020, he died in Chennai from post COVID-19 complications.[23]
